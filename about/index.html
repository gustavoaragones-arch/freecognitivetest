FreeCognitiveTest.org is an independent educational platform providing accessible cognitive screening tools and brain training exercises.

All tools are designed for informational and self-assessment purposes only. They are not intended to diagnose, treat, or replace professional medical evaluation.

This platform is owned and operated by Albor Digital LLC, a product studio based in the United States.
