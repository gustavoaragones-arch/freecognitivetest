FreeCognitiveTest.org is developed and maintained by Albor Digital LLC, an independent digital product studio focused on educational tools and accessible web applications.

This project focuses on creating simple, low-bandwidth cognitive screening tools for public education. The tools are designed for informational purposes only and are not a medical diagnosis.

The goal is to make cognitive awareness tools accessible globally, across languages and devices, without barriers such as accounts, payments, or downloads.
