Intro

FreeCognitiveTest.org focuses on simple design, clear language, and low-bandwidth delivery.

This site is part of a small network of cognitive health tools developed by Albor Digital LLC. The network includes FreeCognitiveTest.org (cognitive screening tools, brain exercises, guides, and printable resources), MemoryTestOnline.org (memory testing and assessment education), and BrainExercisesForSeniors.com (cognitive exercises and brain training for older adults). Together these sites focus on cognitive screening tools and brain training resources.
