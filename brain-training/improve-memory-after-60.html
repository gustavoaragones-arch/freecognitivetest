Improve Memory After 60

Actionable strategies to strengthen recall and attention in later life.

Intro

Memory outcomes often improve when cognitive training is paired with healthy daily habits.

Educational Content

Prioritize sleep, hearing correction, movement, and regular brain activity. Use memory tools that become slightly harder over time. Keep goals realistic and track progress weekly.

FAQ

Does multitasking hurt memory?

Frequent multitasking can reduce encoding quality and increase forgetfulness.

Should I track progress?

Yes, a simple weekly score log helps identify improvement trends.
