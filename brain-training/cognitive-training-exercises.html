Cognitive Training Exercises

Build a balanced weekly plan with varied cognitive challenges.

Intro

Training is most effective when exercises target multiple thinking skills over time.

Educational Content

Combine recall tasks, reaction tasks, and pattern tasks in a repeating weekly plan. Keep records of score, speed, and error rate to monitor progress and detect changes early.

FAQ

How many days per week?

3 to 5 days per week is a practical range.

Is rest important?

Yes, rest days help reduce fatigue and improve consistency.
