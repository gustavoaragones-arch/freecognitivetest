Direct answer

You will memorize three words, complete a short distractor task, recall the words, then draw a clock face showing a specific time. Scoring rules differ by setting; use this tool to learn the flow—not to self-diagnose.

How it works

Open the interactive test, follow on-screen prompts in order, and submit each step when instructed. The page calculates a simple outcome for learning purposes. A clinician would integrate timing, observation, and medical context.

What results mean

Any score or message shown here is a rough educational indicator. False positives and false negatives are possible. Do not change medications or care plans based on this page—discuss concerns with a qualified professional.

FAQ

Is this the official Mini-Cog?

It follows the same task structure for learning; it is not a certified medical device.

How long does it take?

Usually about 3–5 minutes if you read instructions carefully.

Can I use it for a family member?

You can explore it together for awareness, but clinical decisions belong with their healthcare team.
