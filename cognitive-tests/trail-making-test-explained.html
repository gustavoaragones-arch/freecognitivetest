Trail Making Test Explained

Understand why this timed sequencing test is used in cognitive screening.

Intro

Trail making asks the user to connect targets in sequence as quickly and accurately as possible.

Educational Content

The test is sensitive to attention, cognitive flexibility, and processing speed. Longer times can suggest need for further evaluation, especially when paired with other concerning findings.

FAQ

Can poor vision affect performance?

Yes, visual and motor factors can influence results.

Is one result enough?

No, interpretation should use broader clinical context.
