Digit Span Test Explained

A beginner-friendly guide to forward and backward digit span tasks.

Intro

Digit span is a classic short test where users repeat number sequences from memory.

Educational Content

Forward trials focus on immediate attention. Backward trials require mental manipulation and are often more sensitive to executive difficulty. Performance is usually described as the longest correct sequence length.

FAQ

Can stress affect scores?

Yes. Anxiety and fatigue can reduce short-term memory performance.

Should I compare scores to others?

Trends over time are often more useful than single comparisons.
