What Is the Mini-Cog Test?

A plain-language overview of one of the most common cognitive screening tools.

Intro

The Mini-Cog combines three-word recall with a clock drawing task. It is brief and easy to administer.

Educational Content

Clinicians use Mini-Cog as an initial screen when memory or thinking concerns are present. Lower scores suggest that a full evaluation may be needed. Results should always be interpreted in clinical context.

FAQ

How long does Mini-Cog take?

Usually 3 to 5 minutes.

Who can use it?

Clinicians, caregivers, and adults for educational screening support.
