Clock Drawing Test Interpretation

How to read clock drawing performance in a practical and careful way.

Intro

Clock drawing is often used in cognitive screening because it tests several thinking skills at once.

Educational Content

Interpretation usually focuses on whether the clock face is complete, numbers are in reasonable order, and hands show the requested time. A single poor clock should trigger follow-up, not immediate conclusions.

FAQ

What time is commonly requested?

11:10 is frequently used in screening versions.

Can I practice this test?

Yes, practice improves familiarity but does not replace clinical interpretation.
